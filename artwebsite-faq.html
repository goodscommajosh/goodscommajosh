sizing & care

How do your shirts fit?

All shirts are printed on Next Level 100% cotton unisex t-shirts. A size guide can be found here.

How should I wash my shirt?

For best results, wash inside out on cold. This will keep the design in good shape for longer.

payment & shipping

Is ordering through your website secure?

Absolutely. All online orders are actually handled through the platform BigCartel.

My order hasn’t shipped yet. When will it ship?

Patience, grasshopper. This is very much a side hustle for me — please understand that I am not a full-time shirt slinger! I mail out orders once a week (typically on Thursday).

How do you ship the shirts?

Orders will be shipped via USPS first class package service with tracking included.

My order shipped but it has been [a long time] and I have not received it. What’s the deal?

If that is the case, please contact me at orders@joshuagoodman.io.

I live outside of the United States. Why does shipping cost so much?

I don’t know, but it is not my doing! Shipping internationally from the US is exorbitantly expensive and unfortunately there is not much I can do to change it. Désolé!

returns

What is your return policy?

Unworn and unwashed shirts may be exchanged for another size. Please email me at orders@joshuagoodman.io if you are looking to to do so.
